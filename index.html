About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate and research/teaching assistant at Drexel University in the Ecological and Evolutionary Signal Processing and Informatics Laboratory (EESI). I was with the Signal Processing and Pattern Recognition Laboratory (SPPRL) at Rowan University. I defended my Masters thesis, Incremental Learning of Concept Drift from Imbalanced Data, on April 20th 2011. My research is primarily in incremental learning from concept drift and class imbalance.
