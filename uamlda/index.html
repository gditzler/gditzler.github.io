General: Working with the UAMLDA

Our group is current recruiting highly motivated PhD students who have interests in applied machine learning and data mining to aspects of health and the life sciences. Students interested in working with me for a masters degree are highly encouraged to have external funding support. Prospective students should apply to the University of Arizona's Graduate Program then email Prof. Ditzler.

If you are not yet at the University of Arizona, I am sorry that I am not able to answer general inquiries about admission and assistantship; please go here for more information about the ECE program at the UofA and application information.
